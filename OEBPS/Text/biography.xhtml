指原莉乃（さしはら・りの）

Sashihara Rino

Born on November 11, 1992. Blood type O. From Oita-ken. In 2007, she passed the AKB48 5th generation member auditions and began activities as an AKB48 member on March 2008. She is currently a main member of the Idol group, HKT48, which is based in Hakata, Fukuoka. In the 2013 AKB48 Sousenkyo, she obtained first place. The song, "Koi Suru Fortune Cookie" where she sang as center position was a big hit and is now one of the songs that represent AKB48.
